Overview

Earnscape ("we", "our", "the app") is a freelance business management app for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is stored.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Earnscape — including clients, projects, tasks, time entries, and invoices — is stored locally on your device. Your data is saved as JSON files in the app's sandboxed document directory.

We do not have access to your data. We cannot view, retrieve, or recover your data if you delete the app or reset your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

Earnscape does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Client or business data you enter into the app
  • Financial information (invoices, rates, earnings)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes

Subscriptions & Payments

Earnscape offers optional in-app subscriptions (monthly and annual) managed entirely through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or Apple ID.

Subscription management, including cancellation and renewal, is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Third-Party Services

Earnscape does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain any third-party SDKs that collect user data.

The only external service the app communicates with is Apple's StoreKit framework for processing in-app purchases.

Future Cloud Sync

We may introduce optional cloud synchronisation in a future update to allow you to sync data across your devices. If implemented, this feature will:

  • Be entirely optional — you can continue using the app with local-only storage
  • Use Apple's iCloud infrastructure, meaning your data remains within Apple's ecosystem
  • Not grant us access to your synced data

We will update this privacy policy before introducing any cloud features and will notify users within the app.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Settings screen. This permanently removes all stored information from your device.

Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Earnscape is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Free Tier
