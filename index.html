SoloPay — Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: 1 May 2026

Privacy Summary: Your data stays on your device. SoloPay is offline-first — all invoices, time entries, clients, and projects are stored locally on your iPhone. Nothing is sent to our servers, and we don't track you.

1. What Data Does SoloPay Collect?

SoloPay is designed to protect your privacy. Here's exactly what we do and don't collect:

What SoloPay stores (on your device only):

  • Clients you add (name, email, phone, company, address, hourly rate)
  • Projects you create (name, description, budget, deadline)
  • Tasks you add (title, due date, priority, notes)
  • Time entries you log (duration, description, project, client)
  • Invoices you generate (invoice number, amounts, client, line items)
  • Business details you set in Settings (business name, address, contact)
  • Your preferences (currency, budget thresholds, notification settings)

What SoloPay does NOT collect or track:

  • No analytics or usage data
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking cookies
  • No location data
  • No camera, photos, or contacts access
  • No browsing history or personal identifiers
  • No IP address logging
  • No third-party tracking SDKs

2. Where Is My Data Stored?

Local device storage: All your data is stored in encrypted form on your iPhone in the app's private sandbox folder, protected by your device's passcode and iOS encryption.

iCloud backups: If you have iCloud+ enabled, your device backups may include app data. This is controlled by Apple's backup system, not by SoloPay. You can disable iCloud backups for SoloPay in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → SoloPay.

No remote servers: SoloPay does not send your data to any SoloPay servers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it (via email, export, or share sheet).

3. When Does My Data Leave My Device?

Your data only leaves your device when you initiate an action:

  • Email an invoice: When you tap "Email Invoice," the PDF is sent via Apple Mail directly to the recipient. We don't route it through our servers.
  • Export records: When you tap "Records Export," a PDF and CSV files are generated and presented via the iOS share sheet — you choose where they go (email, Messages, Notes, Files, etc.).
  • Share via iOS share sheet: Any share action uses Apple's native share APIs — data goes to the app you select.
  • In-app purchases: When you subscribe to SoloPay Pro, your payment is processed by Apple's StoreKit framework. SoloPay does not see your payment details.

4. Third-Party Services

SoloPay integrates only with Apple's own services:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Apple StoreKit 2 Subscription purchases (Pro tier) Apple receives your purchase details; we don't
UserNotifications Local reminders (invoices, budgets) Notifications stay on your device
MessageUI Email composer for invoice delivery Your email app handles recipient data
UIActivityViewController iOS share sheet Data goes to the app you choose

No analytics, no tracking, no ads. We do not use Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any analytics platform.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Edit: You can edit or update any client, project, invoice, or time entry at any time
  • Export: You can export Records Export (PDF + CSVs) for any UK tax year
  • Delete: You can delete any individual record (client, project, invoice, time entry)
  • Reset all: In Settings → Data, you can "Reset All Data" which permanently deletes everything

UK GDPR & EU GDPR Rights: Under GDPR Article 15–22, you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, export, and port your data. Because SoloPay stores everything on your device and you control it entirely, these rights are exercised directly in the app — no need to contact us.

6. Children's Privacy

SoloPay is designed for freelancers and sole traders (adults). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact focusflowuk@gmail.com and we will help delete their data.

If a material change affects your rights, we will provide notice in the app or via update notes in the App Store.
